Hey! Thanks for your interest in my art. It's great to have you here.

With almost 10 years of photography experience, you'd maybe think I'd have developed a niche by now. Instead, I'm only just starting to figure out what kind of photography inspires me most. I have prior experience with portraits for graduation, engagement, maternity, wedding, prom, family gathering, headshot, and even pets.

Since I'm from a small beach town, I'm comfortable shooting at the beach and in the redwood forests nearby. After traveling the country and visiting other areas of the world as well, I value a diversity of locations and subjects.

I'd love to connect with you if you'd love to connect with me. You can reach me at kayleeegreeen@gmail.com, looking forward to hearing from you!
