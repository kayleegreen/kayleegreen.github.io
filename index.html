Hey! Thanks for stopping by.

I'm so happy you stumbled across my portfolio of work.

With almost 10 years of photography experience, I've met and worked with all sorts of people and subjects. I specialize in group photos, portraits and outdoor shoots. Things I've shot previously include weddings, maternity, engagement, prom, graduation, senior portraits, and even pets.

Growing up in the Bay Area, I had the luxury of shooting in photogenic settings like at the beach and in the beautiful redwoods, and this blessing taught me how much more goes into a photo besides the subject and the background. I have since applied this concept to further challenging my skills and abilities as I attempt to push the boundaries of stardard angles, lighting, posing, etc.

I believe that photos are so valuable because they not only capture important moments but also give us the ability to relive the emotions these moments evoked. And this quality alone makes photography, to me, priceless.

If you like what you see and feel that my style is what you're looking for, what are you waiting for?! Send me a message! I'd love to hear more about why you value photos, too!
